About Us

Sauti Initiative is a dynamic, youth-led organization born from the vision of local university and college students, committed to revolutionizing Tana River County.

We harness natural resources and human potential through sustainable innovation to tackle pressing challenges.

We focus on tackling critical issues that have long hindered our region's progress like climate change, drought, food and water shortages, as well as harmful practices like FGM and early marriages.

We have developed a portfolio of groundbreaking solutions tailored to our local context, each designed to create a meaningful impact.

While we eagerly await the financial backing to fully implement these initiatives, our determination remains unshaken. We have successfully implemented a mentorship and female hygiene program at Charkungu Primary School, funded entirely through member contributions.

As we stand on the cusp of realizing our full potential, we invite partners, supporters, and visionaries to join us in this transformative journey.
