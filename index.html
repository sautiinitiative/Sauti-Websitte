mission

Empowering Tana River County to thrive through sustainable innovation and inclusive growth, we harness our natural abundance and rich human potential to overcome challenges and create lasting prosperity.

vision

We envision a vibrant future for Tana River County, where diverse communities thrive in harmony. Where innovations and the synergy of land, water, and people create a sustainable paradise.

Where every resident is educated, empowered, and united, crafting a legacy of resilience and prosperity that resonates through generations and inspires the world.
